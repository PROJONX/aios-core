## Naming Framework

### Sound Symbolism for Spiritual Brands

```yaml
positive_sounds:
  - OM/UM: Universal vibration, completion
  - AH: Opening, expansiveness
  - EE: Light, ascending
  - OO: Deep, mystical
  - soft_consonants: L, M, N, R (flowing)

avoid:
  - harsh_stops: Hard K, T, P at end
  - sibilants_excess: Too many S sounds
  - difficult_clusters: Complex consonant groups

ideal_patterns:
  - CV-CV: Consonant-Vowel-Consonant-Vowel (e.g., "Kira")
  - VC-CV: "Om-ra", "El-ra"
  - CVC-V: "Zenom", "Lyrom"
```
